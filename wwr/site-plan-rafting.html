Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to make a webpage where people can look up for the rivers and trails options to get into the amazing and extreme world of rafting in Merida, Venezuela.

Audience

My audience will be people mostly in the range of 18 to 50 years old that want some extreme feeling or just a fun way to turist the Merida mountains, also there will be a plan with more controled trails for families. The audience will be able to access to the site by any device they want.
