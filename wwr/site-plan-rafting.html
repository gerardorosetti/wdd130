Overview

Purpose

Mérida, Venezuela is a place with a lot of potential in its nature, with incredible mountains, beautiful forests and an incredible number of ways to take advantage of everything mentioned above, unfortunately none of these incredible features are being used, so to start using these incredible benefits that our land is giving us, we can establish that our purpose is to provide the experience of the incredible nature and rivers here in Merida, Venezuela, while giving to the people the opportunity to do rafting in an extreme and fun way by going through the rapids that cross the fantastic forests of the Merida’s mountains. By giving all this amazing experience to the people we also want to improve the sightseeing here in the country and specifically in the city and the places and towns around it, so we can bring alive once more the activity, the tourism and even improve the sportive live with our rafting, and so let the people around all the world to have the chance to know and admire the unrivaled and unique preciousness of the land we live spreading the Merida’s happiness to everyone that wants to take this amazing adventure with our fantastic rapids.

Audience

My audience will be people mostly in the range of 18 to 50 years old that want some extreme feeling or just a fun way to turist the Merida mountains, also there will be a plan with more controled trails for families. The audience will be able to access to the site by any device they want.
